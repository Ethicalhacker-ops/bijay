My Story

I am Jay Prasad Majhi, a multifaceted professional with a diverse background spanning technology, business, and education. My journey began with a foundation in Electronics and Communication Engineering, which sparked my passion for technology and innovation.

Over the years, I've worn many hats - from Research Engineer to Accountant, Documentation Officer to IT Consultant. This varied experience has given me a unique perspective that allows me to bridge the gap between technical solutions and business needs.

In 2021, I founded Crunch Tech Pvt. Ltd. in Birtamode, Nepal, with the vision of providing comprehensive IT and business solutions to our community. Our services range from domain registration and web hosting to study abroad consulting and IELTS/PTE preparation.

When I'm not working with clients, you can find me publishing football news, mentoring students, or exploring new technologies that can benefit our services. I believe in continuous learning and sharing knowledge to help others succeed.
