IELTS/PTE Preparation

Our specialized test preparation courses are designed to help you achieve your target scores in IELTS or PTE exams. Taught by experienced instructors, our courses cover all test components with a focus on your weak areas. We provide authentic practice materials, simulated tests, and personalized feedback to maximize your performance.

The IELTS program focuses on developing skills for Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking modules, with particular attention to academic vocabulary and test strategies. Our PTE course emphasizes the computer-based format's unique requirements, including speaking into a microphone and integrated skills tasks. Both programs include regular mock tests under exam conditions to build stamina and reduce test anxiety.

We offer flexible learning options including intensive 2-week crash courses, standard 4-6 week programs, and extended 8-12 week courses for beginners. Our small class sizes (max 8 students) ensure individual attention, and we provide detailed progress reports after each test. Additional resources include access to our online practice portal, one-on-one tutoring sessions, and post-course support until you achieve your desired score.
